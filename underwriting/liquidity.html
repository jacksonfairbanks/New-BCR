Why Liquidity Matters for BTC Treasury

For large treasury operations, understanding market liquidity is crucial. Poor liquidity can lead to significant slippage during large buy/sell orders, impact NAV calculations, and create execution risk during volatile periods. This analysis helps assess the market's ability to absorb treasury-sized transactions.
